# Design Overview

## GUI
- **Main Screen**: Displays the current video playing from YouTube, with play/pause and next video controls.
- **Video Player**: Shows video playback, along with AI insights on the current video.
- **AI Information Display**: Shows predictions or analysis from the AI model on the video content.

## AI Model
The AI model will analyze video frames, detecting objects or actions. This will be achieved through deep learning models like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
